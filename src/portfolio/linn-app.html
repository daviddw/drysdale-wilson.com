Linn App
Moving beyond the basics
The objective for the Linn app was to make using a Linn music system a transformational user experience.
The Linn app would achieve market differentiation by providing,
  1. quick access to favourite music,
  2. a music discovery journey, and
  3. detailed information on music of interest
Modes of Operation
Using our domain knowledge and the data collected from our previous control software we were able to conclude that a user interacted with our control software in 4 different ways.
  • Quick - user wishes to get audio playing as quickly as possible
  • Discovery - user wishes to go on a journey of discovery whilst using their Linn music system
  • Detailed - user wishes to find out detailed information about particular pieces of music as they are browsing
  • Multi-room - user owns multiple Linn music systems and wishes to be able to easily listen to music on any of their systems
From these modes of operation primary and secondary use cases were created.
